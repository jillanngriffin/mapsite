  • From One Entrepreneur to Another
  • Entrepreneurs typically go their own way. They build a business from their own great ideas. Over time, being independent and self-sufficient doesn’t always work to your advantage. MAP knows where entrepreneurs need to spend their time. In fact, that’s our coaching focus.

    When we handle the details, you can concentrate on profitability. Coaching involves where you want to go and how to get there — not just where you’ve been. By design, MAP focuses on your needs and wants with:

    • Evaluation. We provide an objective, third-party evaluation of your operation. Our knowledgeable coaching can quickly identify your strengths and weaknesses.
    • Implementation. As your sounding board, we can help organize from chaos and establish accountability.
    • Regular Review. Once you’re on the right track, regular reviews can keep you on the right path and open to unfolding opportunities.
